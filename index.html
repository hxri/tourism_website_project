About

The Institute of Management in Kerala was established under the tutelage of the University of Kerala in the year 1991. The institute is part of the School of Business Management and Legal studies, University of Kerala. The vision of the institute is to create a generation of powerful, value driven individuals who could take humanity to the most glorious of civilizations. The institute aims through its curriculum and pedagogy to create a manager comprising of positive values such as responsibility, creativity and integrity. IMK offers MBA (General), MBA (Travel and Tourism), and MBA regular (Evening) through CSS mode. IMK also offers PhD and PDF in Management studies. IMK has illustrious alumni and has extended their Evening programme to HLL Management Academy and ACSTI

YATHRA is an inter-collegiate Tourism Management fest organized by MBA Tourism Students of the Institute of Management in Kerala (IMK), University of Kerala, Kariyavattom campus. The slogan of Yathra is "Wings to explore".The main aim of the Yathra fest is to enhance the students' skills and mould the budding managers. The eventÂ provides a platform for students to showcase one's unique talents and are essential in the holistic development of the students' personality. The fest consists of cultural and academic level programs in which postgraduate and undergraduate students could participate.
